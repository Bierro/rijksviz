Faces of the Rijks

Explore the individuals in the paintings of the Rijksmuseum through their social media pages.
Using a facial recognition API, Kairos, we identified all of the faces within the Rijksmuseum’s paintings. We playfully brought them to life by giving each of the “individuals” a social media page, where we listed their “personal information” such as birthday, nationality, and skin texture. By clicking through their “friends,” or faces randomly selected from other paintings, the user is led to browse new pages and can traverse the museum's collection.
