Faces of the Rijks

We identified all of the faces within the Rijksmuseum’s paintings and gave each of the “individuals” a personal social media page.
By clicking through their “friends,” or faces randomly selected from other paintings, the user is led to browse new pages and can traverse the museum's collection. To accomplish this, we used a facial recognition API called Kairos.
