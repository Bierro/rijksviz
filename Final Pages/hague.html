Hague School

Each painting on display from the Hague School is broken down by its pixels and displayed as a string of colors sorted by hue.
This focuses in on the painters of the Hague School and enables users to compare and explore their work through their paintings' colors and dimensions. Each painting is summarized by a strip of its colors ordered by hue; each painting is also stacked on top of one another with their bottom left corners lined-up.
These two visualizations are connected with interactivity so users can identify which dimensions pair with which colors, allowing users to analyze the paintings in a new light.
