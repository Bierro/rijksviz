Beyond the Exhibits

All of the Rijksmusem's 4,000 paintings ordered by their creation date and grouped by
whether they are currently on display (above the line) or in the museum's storage facilities.
This visualization features all of the Rijksmuseum’s 4,000 paintings, ordered by century. The top half of the chart displays the paintings currently on display, while the bottom half displays the paintings in storage. It becomes immediately clear that visiting the museum’s exhibits only scratches the surface of the impressive collection.
Looking forward, we are interested in expanding this section to empower users to browse archived work further. Revealing stored art provides an exciting opportunity because it is one of the few clear advantages technology has over an in-person museum experience.
